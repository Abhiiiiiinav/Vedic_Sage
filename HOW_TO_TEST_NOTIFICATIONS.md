# How to Test Notifications - Quick Guide

## 🎯 Two Ways to Access the Test Screen

### Method 1: Direct Access (Fastest)
1. Open app drawer (tap ☰ hamburger menu)
2. Scroll down to bottom section
3. Tap **"Test All Notifications"** (purple icon with science beaker 🧪)
4. You're now in the testing lab!

### Method 2: Via Settings
1. Open app drawer (tap ☰ hamburger menu)
2. Tap **"Notification Settings"** (purple bell icon 🔔)
3. Tap **"Test All Types"** button (cyan button)
4. You're now in the testing lab!

## 🧪 In the Testing Lab

You'll see:
- **6 Notification Type Cards** - Tap any to test individually
- **Scheduled Notifications Info** - Shows when automatic notifications fire
- **"Test All Notifications" Button** - Sends all 6 at once

### Individual Tests
Tap any card to test that notification type:
- 🔥 **Streak Alert** (Orange) - "Your 7-day streak is at risk!"
- 📚 **Learning Reminder** (Green) - "Continue your Jyotish journey"
- ☀️ **Cosmic Update** (Cyan) - "Your daily cosmic forecast is ready"
- 👥 **Social Notification** (Purple) - "New friend request"
- 🏆 **Achievement Alert** (Gold) - "Achievement Unlocked!"
- ✨ **Daily Tasks** (Purple) - "Your cosmic tasks are ready!"

### Test All at Once
1. Scroll to bottom of test screen
2. Tap **"Test All Notifications"** (purple gradient button with rocket 🚀)
3. All 6 notifications will be sent
4. Check your notification center!

## 📱 Where to See Results

### In-App Notification Center
1. Tap the **bell icon (🔔)** at the top of the home screen
2. See all notifications listed
3. Each has custom icon and color
4. Tap to view details
5. Swipe to dismiss

### Device Notifications (Android)
1. Pull down notification shade from top of screen
2. See system notifications with custom icons
3. Tap to open app
4. Swipe to dismiss

## ✅ What You Should See

After testing, you should have:
- ✅ Notifications in the in-app notification center
- ✅ Device notifications in Android notification shade
- ✅ Custom icons and colors for each type
- ✅ Success message at bottom of test screen
- ✅ Green checkmark confirmation

## 🔄 Hot Reload

If you don't see the test options:
1. Press **'r'** in terminal to hot reload
2. Or press **'R'** to hot restart
3. Or close and reopen the app

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Test in Different States**
   - App in foreground (you'll see in-app notifications)
   - App in background (you'll see device notifications)
   - App closed (device notifications persist)

2. **Check Both Places**
   - In-app notification center (bell icon)
   - Device notification shade (pull down from top)

3. **Test Individual Types First**
   - Helps you understand each notification
   - See different icons and colors
   - Learn what each type is for

4. **Then Test All**
   - Sends all 6 at once
   - Great for seeing them all together
   - Tests notification grouping

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the test option"
- Hot reload the app (press 'r' in terminal)
- Or restart the app completely
- Check you're looking in the right section (bottom of drawer)

### "Notifications don't appear"
- Check device notification permissions
- Settings → Apps → AstroLearn → Notifications → Enable
- Disable Do Not Disturb mode
- Check battery optimization settings

### "Only in-app notifications work"
- This is normal if app is in foreground
- Put app in background to see device notifications
- Or close app completely and test again
