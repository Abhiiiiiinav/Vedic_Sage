# Divisional Charts (Varga Charts) - Complete Guide

## Overview

Divisional charts, also known as Varga charts or Amsas, are one of the most powerful tools in Vedic astrology. They provide detailed insights into specific areas of life by dividing the zodiac into precise segments.

## What are Divisional Charts?

The birth chart (D1/Rasi) shows the general life path, but divisional charts reveal the finer details. Each chart divides the zodiac differently to focus on specific life areas.

## The 16 Main Divisional Charts (Shodasha Vargas)

These are the most commonly used charts in Vedic astrology:

### 1. D1 - Rasi (Birth Chart) ⭐
- **Division**: 1 (no division)
- **Significations**: Physical body, personality, general life path
- **Importance**: Foundation chart - all other charts are derived from this

### 2. D2 - Hora ⭐
- **Division**: 2 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Wealth, prosperity, financial matters, movable assets
- **Usage**: Analyzing earning capacity and financial fortune

### 3. D3 - Drekkana ⭐
- **Division**: 3 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Siblings, courage, short journeys, communication
- **Usage**: Understanding relationship with brothers/sisters

### 4. D4 - Chaturthamsa
- **Division**: 4 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Fortune, property, fixed assets, mother, home
- **Usage**: Real estate, inheritance, maternal influences

### 5. D7 - Saptamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 7 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Children, progeny, creativity, grandchildren
- **Usage**: Fertility, relationship with children

### 6. D9 - Navamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 9 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Marriage, spouse, dharma, spiritual path, fortune
- **Importance**: Second most important chart after D1
- **Usage**: Marriage compatibility, spiritual evolution, strength of planets

### 7. D10 - Dasamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 10 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Career, profession, status, achievements, authority
- **Usage**: Professional success, career path, public recognition

### 8. D12 - Dwadasamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 12 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Parents, lineage, ancestors, past life connections
- **Usage**: Understanding parental influences and ancestral karma

### 9. D16 - Shodasamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 16 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Vehicles, comforts, happiness, luxuries, conveyances
- **Usage**: Material comforts, vehicles, general happiness

### 10. D20 - Vimsamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 20 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Spiritual progress, worship, religious activities
- **Usage**: Spiritual inclinations, devotion, religious practices

### 11. D24 - Chaturvimsamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 24 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Education, learning, knowledge, academic success
- **Usage**: Educational achievements, learning abilities

### 12. D27 - Saptavimsamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 27 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Strengths, weaknesses, vitality, energy levels
- **Usage**: Physical and mental strength analysis

### 13. D30 - Trimsamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 30 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Misfortunes, evils, suffering, calamities
- **Usage**: Understanding challenges and how to overcome them

### 14. D40 - Khavedamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 40 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Auspicious effects, maternal legacy, blessings
- **Usage**: Maternal influences, general auspiciousness

### 15. D45 - Akshavedamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 45 parts per sign
- **Significations**: General indications, character, overall nature
- **Usage**: Character analysis, general behavior patterns

### 16. D60 - Shashtyamsa ⭐
- **Division**: 60 parts per sign
- **Significations**: Past life karma, overall life synthesis
- **Importance**: Most detailed chart - shows complete karmic picture
- **Usage**: Understanding past life influences, complete life analysis

## Additional Divisional Charts (D5-D59)

The remaining charts (D5, D6, D8, D11, D13-D15, D17-D19, D21-D23, D25-D29, D31-D39, D41-D44, D46-D59) provide even more specific insights into various life areas. While less commonly used, they can offer valuable details for in-depth analysis.

## How to Use Divisional Charts

1. **Start with D1**: Always analyze the birth chart first
2. **Check D9**: Navamsa is crucial for understanding planetary strength
3. **Focus on Relevant Charts**: Use specific charts based on the question:
   - Marriage → D9
   - Career → D10
   - Children → D7
   - Wealth → D2
   - Education → D24

4. **Look for Patterns**: Planets strong in multiple charts show consistent results
5. **Vargottama**: Planets in the same sign in D1 and divisional charts are very powerful

## Planetary Strength in Divisional Charts

A planet is considered strong when it:
- Occupies its own sign or exaltation sign in divisional charts
- Is in the same sign in multiple divisional charts (Vargottama)
- Receives aspects from benefics in divisional charts
- Is placed in favorable houses (1, 4, 5, 7, 9, 10)

## Important Notes

- **Quality over Quantity**: Focus on the 16 main charts rather than all 60
- **Context Matters**: Always interpret divisional charts in context of D1
- **Timing**: Use Dasha periods to time events shown in divisional charts
- **Synthesis**: Combine insights from multiple charts for accurate predictions
